INTRODUCTION

Akim Champion Enterprise is a 100% Bumiputera owned company.
The company has been formed on 10th June 2013.
The Principal activities of the company are Civil & Structure,
Mechanical & Electrical including Track Works, Transmission Tower,
Demolition Works, Renovation and Interior Design, Infrastructural
and Maintenance Works.
In order to meet challenges ahead, the company was converted
into a limited company with the Board of Director.

We have upgraded our G2 to G4 with the Construction Industry
Development Board (CIDB). With our engineering capability and
experiences, we have been able to effectively execute and deliver
projects to the satisfaction of clients.
We look forward to improve our facilities and services to meet future
requirement of our clients.
